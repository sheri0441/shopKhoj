Shopping in Delhi Markets

Bunder Street

Delhi is the most exciting city to shop in India.The sheer range of products that is available in Delhi Markets is amazing. From the best Indian designer wear bridal dresses to simple cotton dresses, ,shopping is amazing . Above all, there are exquisite jewellery and imitation jewellery. Further, accessories are stylish & trendy.

Shopping for Designer Wear

The Emporio Mall in Vasant Kunj is the shopping mall for Designer Wear dresses. In addition, they stock Indian, western & Indo wetsern dresses .For example, the top floor of the mall,is reserved for Indian designers. All the designers are very well known & popular.Tarun Tahiliani, Suneet Verma, Rohit Bal,Ritu Kumar & so on. So,one can shop for designer bridal lehengas,sarees & salwar suits .In addition, the Emporio mall has some of the best international luxury brands. Louis Vuitton, Jimmy Choo, Salvatore Faragamo, and Cartier are a few. This Delhi shopping mall is indeed a combination of the best Indian designer wear & the best of international brands

More of luxury shopping

Another market in Delhi to shop for designer wear is the Defence Colony Market.This market is slowly but surely attracting popular Indian designers. The Shahpur Jat Market is a shopping market for upcoming designers. One can pick up bridal lehengas, sarees and gowns at very good prices in this market.

Shopping @ Chandni Chowk Market

Lets introduce the shops in the oldest shopping market in Delhi ,Chandni Chowk Market.Firstly, it is a wholesale market for all groceries. However, several people shop here for a range of fabrics, bridal trousseau, wedding cards,books & more. Shop for silver jewellery in Dariba Kalan.In addition, shop for Banarsi ,Chanderi, georgette and cotton fabrics. Attar or perfumed oil,exotic soaps and potpouri is also available. It is a once in a lifetime experience .Go in a rickshaw in Chandni Chowk and check out all the fun. It is noisy.

Shopping in South Delhi Market

As compared to Chandini Chowk, the South Extension Market,Khan Market and the GK(Greater Kailash Market) shops are less crowded. They have a mix of western wear ,bridal wear & accessories shops. Best shops for bridal wear in Delhi are Frontier Raas,Ritu Kumar ,Meena Bazar and L'affaire.Nalli, Kalpana and Rasi Silks are some of the best shops for sarees.

The Janpath Shopping market and Sarojini Nagar shopping and Kamala Nagar Markets are popular with the youngsters.

State Government Emporia are in CP,(Connaught Place). The best textiles ,jewellery & handicrafts from States are available.
